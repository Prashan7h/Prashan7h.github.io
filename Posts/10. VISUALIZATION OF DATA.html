#generating data 
mySamples= []
myLinear= []
myQuadratic= []
myCubic= []
myExponential= []

for i in range(0, 30):
    mySamples.append(i)
    myLinear.append(i)
    myQuadratic.append(i**2)
    myCubic.append(i**3)
    myExponential.append(1.5**i)

  • in particular, the scales on the graphs are very different

  • a second option is to plot multiple functions on the same display
